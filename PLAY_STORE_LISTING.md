## Full description (max 4000 characters)
Reality Check is where you go to think for yourself.

Every day we're told what to believe. Reality Check does the opposite — it takes a claim and shows you the full spectrum of perspectives behind it: left, right, historical, scientific, and contrarian. No agenda. Just angles.

WHY REALITY CHECK
• Five lenses on every claim — see how different worldviews interpret the same issue
• Backed by real sources — every perspective is labeled, color-coded, and cited
• No echo chamber — we surface the strongest version of every side, not just the popular one
• Built for curious minds — politics, science, health, history, economy, media, and technology

HOW IT WORKS
1. Browse trending claims the community is debating right now
2. Tap any claim to see multiple expert-backed perspectives
3. Read the sources, weigh the angles, and draw your own conclusion
4. Join the discussion and submit claims of your own

GO DEEPER WITH PREMIUM
• Unlimited claim checks
• Full historical context on every issue
• Saved research and reading history

Reality Check isn't here to tell you what's true. It's here to make sure you've seen every side before you decide.

Download Reality Check and start seeing the whole picture.
